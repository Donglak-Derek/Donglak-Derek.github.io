Mobile App Design

Fintech Banking App Redesign

Reimagining the mobile banking experience for the digital-native generation.

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Timeline

3 Weeks

Tools

Figma, Photoshop, After Effects

The Challenge

Users found the existing banking application cluttered and difficult to navigate. Key features like transfers and account settings were buried under multiple menus, leading to frustration and high drop-off rates.


Goals

  • Simplify the navigation structure.
  • Modernize the visual language.
  • Improve accessibility for all users.

The Process

I started by analyzing competitor apps and conducting user interviews to understand pain points. Based on the insights, I created low-fidelity wireframes to test navigation flows.

The Solution

The new design introduces a "Quick Action" floating button for common tasks, a cleaner dashboard with customizable widgets, and a dark mode option.

Outcome & Learnings

Initial testing showed a 40% reduction in time-to-task for money transfers. I learned the importance of accounting for edge cases in financial data visualization.
